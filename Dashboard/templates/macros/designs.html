About

The Pronto Dashboard is a task-tracking and allocation system for SATS Interline officers to monitor the status of ground operations for SQ flights. It provides a platform to check flight schedule, track their drivers, and allocate drivers to specific tasks for HOT transfer baggage. It aims to improve and log the allocation process as well as provide much-needed visibility of the ground operations, in order to alleviate baggage mishandling. This application is designed by students of the SUTD, in collaboration with the SIA Ground Service Department, as part of SUTD's capstone project.
